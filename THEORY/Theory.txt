Octave is doubling of frequency.
People can hear near eleven octaves.
People hear from 16-20 Hz to 16000-20000-22000 Hz.
Normal sound reduces for 3 Db per octave.
So normal sound on octave #11 is lesser for 33 Db than on octave #1.
Microphones, headphones, speakers, room and ears are
equalising the sound, changing it's spectrum.
One octave contains 12 notes / intervals / semitones.
One semitone is Root12(2) = 1.0594630943592953;
